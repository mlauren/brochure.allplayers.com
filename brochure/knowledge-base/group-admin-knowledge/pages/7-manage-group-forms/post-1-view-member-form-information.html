View member form information

As a group administrator, you can view form information your members submit.

To view member form information, click More Tools > Create Form in your Group Toolbox.


On your Manage your group forms page, you can view all of the forms associated with your group.

To view the form information your members have submitted, click Results next to the form that you would like to review.


On the results page, you see an overview of the submissions for the form you selected. From here, you can view individual submissions or export some or all of the submissions as a file.


View individual form submission

Click View next to the member that you would like to review.


Here you can view all of the information that a specific member submitted to the selected form. You can also navigate forward and backward through the other submissions with the navigation near the top of the page.


Export form submissions

Click Download at the top of the page.


Choose the format for the export as either Microsoft Excel or Delimited text.

If you choose delimited text, select the desired delimiter from the pulldown menu. The default delimiter is a tab.


Under Select List Options, choose how you would like the exported list to display.

You can select full or abbreviated list form fields, and either multi-column or condensed information fields.


Under Included Export Components, choose what components of the form you would like to include in the export.

You can select any combination of the fields from your form, or simply select Include all components to export the entire form.


Under Download Range Options, select the range of submissions you would like to include in your export.


Once you have completed your selections, click Download to start the export of your form submissions.

Large forms and forms with a large number of submissions may require some time to export.
