Set up "no fee" group registration

Once you have created your group at AllPlayers.com, you'll want members to join your group!

To allow people to join your group, you will need to enable registration. With AllPlayers.com, you can have free group registration, or you can require that members pay a fee to join the group.

In this section, we will focus on enabling registration for a group that does not require fees to join.

While there are many ways to set up registration for leagues and teams, in this example, we will enable registration for teams in our sample organization to allow players, parents and fans to join a team.

Navigate to the group that you would like members to join.

Select More Tools > Registration in your Group Toolbox.

Registration Begins is the day you want registration to be open on AllPlayers.com for your potential members.

If you want members to be able to join your group as soon as you set up registration, choose today's date.

If registration for your group opens on a particular day in the future, for example, the publicized day that members can sign up or the day of your coaches meeting, choose that date.

Select the Registration Button Text that you want to display on your registration button. When registration is enabled for your group, visitors will click that button to join your group.

We recommend Register for groups that require a registration fee, and Join for groups that are free to join.

Choose the Roles that you want to enable for your group.

By default, the predefined roles that you choose from may include Coach, Player, Volunteer, Fan or more, depending on the category of your group.

×

You can change the name of any default roles to suit the needs of your group. In your Group Toolbox under More Tools, choose Members. Click Roles in the navigation bar, then click Edit Roles. Click the edit link for a role to change its name.

Configure the settings for each Role that you are enabling:

  • Registration Form: If your group needs to collect data from your members as they register for your group, you can Add a Registration Form to request this information. If your group is related to other groups that already have registration forms, you can choose a preexisting form.
  • Minimum/Maximum Age: If a role is age-restricted, choose you minimum and maximum ages. TIP: If your league allows players to "play up" to the next age level, ensure that your minimum age is set to allow younger players to join the group.
  • Gender Requirement: Restrict your role to allow only males or females in a given role (for example, players in a girls' basketball league) if necessary.
  • Registration Limit: If a maximum number of people are allowed in a role, enter that number. Example: enter 10 for Player role if only 10 players are allowed on a team; enter 1 for Coach role if only one coach is allowed per team.
  • Direct Role Registration URL: This link is automatically generated; you should not alter this link.
  • Hidden registration for [role]: If you do not want to show a role on your registration page for your group, check this box. Then provide the Direct Role Registration URL to the person(s) you want to register for this role. Example: if you want a member to have a certain role, but do not want to display that role to all members, use Hidden registration for [role].
  • Enable challenge code for [role]: If you only want certain people to register for a specific role in your group, enable the challenge code and enter a code of your choice. Provide this code to a user to restrict registration to only those who know the code. Example: if you want to only allow people who you have cleared to be a coach to assume the Coach role, set a challenge code for that role. Provide the challenge code to your coaches so that only they can join the group in the Coach role.

Check the Subgroups box if you are enabling registration for a group, and you want users to select a subgroup to join.

For example, if you are setting up registration for a league with age division subgroups, enabling this feature will allow users to select their age division when they sign up for the league.

Check the Enable Group Registration box if you allow groups (like a team) to join your group.

Use this feature if you are a league that does not accept individual player registrations, but only team registrations.

Do not use this feature if you only accept individual registrations.

If your group requires a member to agree to any terms or conditions when they join your group, enter that text in the General Release field.

Save Configuration when you are finished.

Enabling Registration

If you have not yet enabled registration for your group, you will be directed to the Features page for your group.

Locate the Registration feature, choose Enabled from the dropdown box, then Save Configuration.

On your group page, you will see a new box appear with either a Register or Join button, depending on the text you chose when you configured your registration settings.
