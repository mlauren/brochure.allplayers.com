If your group has been approved to accept payments through AllPlayers.com, you can accept donations and contributions from your AllPlayers.com group pages.

Display donations box on your group's dashboard

Click Design Page with Dashboard in your Group Toolbox to show your Dashboard.

Choose Store from the Add dropdown list.


Click and drag the Donate block box onto your page.

See Add boxes to your page for more information on adding elements to your page with your dashboard.


When you have placed the Donate block box on your page, click Save Changes in your Dashboard.

If you would like to change where you have placed your box, see Move boxes around on your page for more information.


You probably don't have a donation product configured yet so the DONATE box on your page will display the text "null". Don't worry! Everything will look beautiful once we've set up a donation product.

Set up donation product

In your Group Toolbox, navigate to Administrative Tools > Store.


Click the Settings tab.


Click Configure Donations.


Complete the Minimum Amount, Maximum Amount, Goal, Suggested Amount, and Suggestion Label fields.


When you're done filling out these fields, click Save product


Click BACK TO {groupname} to return to your group's dashboard.


You will now be back on your group's dashboard and your DONATE box will be populated.


To encourage your members to donate to your group, create a custom box in the main content area of your group page explaining how contributions will benefit your group and members. To learn more about custom boxes, click here.
