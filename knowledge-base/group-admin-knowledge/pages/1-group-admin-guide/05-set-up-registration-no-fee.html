Once you have created your group at AllPlayers.com, you'll want members to join your group!

To allow people to join your group, you will need to enable registration. With AllPlayers.com, you can have free registration, or you can require that members pay a fee to join the group.

In this section, we will focus on enabling registration for a group that does not require fees to join.

While there are many ways to set up registration for leagues and teams, in this example, we will enable registration to allow players, parents and fans to join a team.

Navigate to the group that you would like members to join.

Select Administrative Tools > Registration in your Group Toolbox.


Click here to learn more about the group toolbox.

Configure Registration Settings

We will start configuring registration for your group by adjusting some of the main settings.

Registration date and time

Adjusting this settings allows registration to automatically be enabled and disabled for this group.

To enable automatic registration, click edit, then check the box next to Automatically Enable/Disable Registration. You can then select the start and end dates and times for registration.

Registration Title

The registration title is the title that registrants will see when registering for your group.

Registration Description

The registration description is the message that is displayed to logged out users when visiting your registration page.

Registration Fees

This will let you know if you have the ability to receive registration fees from registrants.

If you see Enabled, you already have the ability to receive registration fees. If you see Requested, you have requested this ability but are not yet able to receive registration fees. If you see Disabled, you can request the ability to receive registration fees by clicking Edit, and then check the box next to Request fee capability.

Registration Button Text

Select the Registration Button Text that you want to display on your registration button. When registration is enabled for your group, visitors will click that button to join your group.

We recommend Register for groups that require a registration fee, and Join for groups that are free to join.

Subgroup registration

Enable if you are enabling registration for a group, and you want users to select a subgroup to join.

For example, if you are setting up registration for a league with age division subgroups, enabling this feature will allow users to select their age division when they sign up for the league.

Allow guardian to register child

Allows a parent/guardian to register their child for the group on the child's behalf.

Guardian Registration Forms

Adds a registration form that a guardian must fill out if they are registering a child for the group.

General release information

If your group requires a member to agree to any terms or conditions when they join your group, enter that text in the General Release field.

Show registration breadcrumb

This allows you to enable or disable the indicator showing the user what step they are in during registration.

In most instances, there is no reason to disable this.

Individual Registration

You can access the Individual Registration page by clicking the tab labeled Individual Registration


The Individual Registration page allows you to set up roles which users can register for.

By default, the predefined roles that you choose from may include Coach, Player, Volunteer, Fan or more, depending on the category of your group.

Each of these roles has multiple settings that you can configure individually. To enable a role and configure its options, click the Off switch next to the role.


You can change the name of any default roles to suit the needs of your group. In your Group Toolbox under Administrative Tools, choose Roles, then click Edit Roles. Click the edit link for a role to change its name.

Registration Form:

If your group needs to collect data from your members as they register for your group, you can Add a Registration Form to request this information. If your group is related to other groups that already have registration forms, you can choose a preexisting form.

Age Requirements

If a role is age-restricted, choose the minimum and maximum ages. TIP: If your league allows players to "play up" to the next age level, ensure that your minimum age is set to allow younger players to join the group.

Gender Requirements

Restrict your role to allow only males or females in a given role (for example, players in a girls' basketball league) if necessary.

Registration Fees

If your group needs to collect a registration fee from its registrants, you can add a registration fee. If your group is related to other groups that already have registration fees, you can choose a preexisting fee.

You can learn more about accepting payments here.

Direct Role Registration URL

Give this link to registrants to bring them directly to this role when registering for your group.

Skip role selection

Enabling this setting will skip the role selection page.

Warning: This should only be used when there is only one role available with no fee attached.

Hide role selection

If you do not want to show a role on your registration page for your group, check this box. Then provide the Direct Role Registration URL to the person(s) you want to register for this role.

Challenge Code

If you only want certain people to register for a specific role in your group, enable the challenge code and enter a code of your choice. Provide this code to a user to restrict registration to only those who know the code. The role will only become visible once the challenge code is entered.

Allow when registering others

For current group members who have the permission to register other users to this group, this role can only be added by them if this option is enabled.

Flag role when paid

For roles with registration fees attached, assign the specified flag to the user when they have completed payment.

Additional role when payment complete

For roles with registration fees attached, assign an additional role to the registrant once they have completed payment.

Do not assign this role until fees are paid

This role will not be assigned to the registrant until the registration fee is paid.

Save Settings and Enable Registration

Once you are finished setting up your roles, click Save configuration.


If you have not yet enabled registration for your group, a window will appear giving you the option to Enable Now or Save and Enable Later.

Click Enable Now


You will be taken back to your group page and you will see a registration button in the registration box.
