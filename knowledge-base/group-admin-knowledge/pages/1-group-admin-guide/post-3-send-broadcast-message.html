As a group administrator of one or more groups on AllPlayers.com, you have the ability to send messages out to any or all of the members of your groups using our Broadcast tool.

Your message will be sent to all of the group members you select. Each member chooses to receive messages as an email, a text message or both, along with receiving the message on the AllPlayers.com message center.

Navigate to the group that you would like to send the broadcast message from.

Select Send a Message in your Group Toolbox.


Click here to learn more about the group toolbox.


Select the Groups you would like to receive your message. Check Select All to send a message to all groups below this one.

If there are no subgroups for your group, this option will not display and you can skip to the next step.


Click Choose your roles.

Select the Roles you would like to receive your message. Click Select All to send a message to all roles in the groups you selected.


Click Compose and send your message.

Compose your message. Notice that your TO: box has been populated with the names of group members who have the role in the groups you selected.

You can remove a specific recipient by clicking the X next to their name.

If you are sending the message to a large number of groups or users, in place of the group members' names, you will see the number of groups and roles that the message is being sent to.

You can send yourself a copy of the message you send by clicking the checkbox next to Send yourself a copy.


Users choose how they want delivery, whether via SMS (text message) or email. Standard text messages are limited to 140 characters. If your message is longer and delivered via SMS, the mobile provider decides how the text message will be delivered, and may truncate your message.


Click Send.

Your message will be queued for delivery to your recipients.


In most instances, your message will be sent from our system, and received by your recipients, within 1-15 minutes, depending on traffic. However, as with any email or text message, we cannot control when the providers of your recipients' email or SMS will deliver the message to your members' inboxes.
