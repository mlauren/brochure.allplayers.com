As a group admin, you have the ability to upload a custom group logo and modify the color scheme of your group's pages.

To change your group's logo and select your group's colors:

Navigate to the group that you want to edit.

In the group toolbox, navigate to Administrative Tools > Group settings

Change colors

To change your group's primary or secondary color, hover over current color, and select the color you would like to replace it with.

Change logo

If you currently have a custom image uploaded, click Remove.

Skip this step if you do not have a custom image uploaded


Click Choose File to pick a file from your computer to use as your group's logo.

Click Upload once you have selected an image.


Once your image has finished uploading, you can crop it to the correct aspect ratio.

Save changes

Once you have changed your group's colors and logo, click Save Group to save your changes.
